
# PRODIGY_DS_01 — World Population Distribution

**Internship:** Prodigy InfoTech | Data Science Track  
**Intern:** Siddhi Sharma | **Tool:** Power BI Desktop  
**Dataset:** World Bank — Population, total (SP.POP.TOTL)

## Objective
Visualize the distribution of world population across countries 
and over time using bar charts and histograms.

## Visuals Created
- Bar Chart: Top 10 most populous countries (2023)
- Histogram: Countries grouped by population size
- Line Chart: World population growth 1960–2023
- Stacked Bar: Population by world region
- KPI Cards: Total world population, number of countries
- Slicer: Interactive year filter

## Key Findings
- India became the most populous country in 2023 (1.43 billion)
- Over 100 countries have populations under 10 million
- World population crossed 8 billion in 2022
- South Asia and East Asia account for the largest share

## Tools Used
Power BI Desktop — Power Query, DAX, Map visuals, Slicers
